Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AyurSutra and Panchkarma treatments

How do I book an appointment?

Booking an appointment is easy! Follow these steps:

  1. Visit our Hospitals page
  2. Search for hospitals in your preferred location
  3. Select a hospital and view available doctors
  4. Click the "Book Appointment" button
  5. Fill in your details including name and phone number
  6. Verify your phone number with OTP
  7. Select your preferred date and time slot
  8. Optionally describe your health concerns
  9. Submit your appointment request

The hospital admin will review your request and assign you to an appropriate doctor. You'll receive login credentials once your appointment is confirmed.

Can I choose my doctor?

While you can view doctor profiles and their specializations, the final doctor assignment is made by the hospital administration based on:

  • Your specific health condition
  • Doctor's availability and expertise
  • Treatment requirements
  • Doctor's current patient load

However, you can express a preference in the appointment booking form or contact the hospital directly to discuss doctor selection.

How is my feedback used?

Your feedback is valuable and is used to:

  • Improve Treatment Quality: Help doctors understand what works best for patients
  • Enhance Service: Improve hospital facilities and staff training
  • Doctor Ratings: Contribute to doctor ratings displayed on hospital profiles
  • System Improvements: Enhance our platform based on user experiences
  • Quality Assurance: Maintain high standards across partner hospitals

All feedback is kept confidential and used only for improvement purposes. You can provide feedback through your patient dashboard after each session.

How does Panchkarma treatment work?

Panchkarma is a comprehensive detoxification and rejuvenation program consisting of five main therapies:

Vaman (Therapeutic Vomiting)

Eliminates excess kapha dosha from the respiratory and digestive systems

Virechan (Purgation)

Removes excess pitta dosha from the liver and digestive system

Basti (Medicated Enema)

Balances vata dosha and cleanses the colon

Nasya (Nasal Administration)

Clears sinuses and treats head-related disorders

Raktamokshana (Bloodletting)

Purifies blood and treats blood-related disorders

The treatment is personalized based on your constitution, health condition, and specific needs. The entire process typically takes 7-21 days depending on your condition.

What if I miss a session?

If you miss a scheduled treatment session:

  1. Contact Immediately: Inform your doctor or hospital as soon as possible
  2. Reschedule: Work with your doctor to reschedule the missed session
  3. Treatment Continuity: Your doctor may adjust the treatment plan to maintain effectiveness
  4. No Penalty: There are no penalties for genuine emergencies

Important: Panchkarma treatments follow a specific sequence and timing. Missing sessions can affect treatment outcomes, so it's crucial to maintain regular attendance. If you anticipate scheduling conflicts, discuss alternatives with your doctor in advance.

How do I reschedule my treatment?

You can reschedule your treatment sessions in several ways:

Through Patient Dashboard:

  1. Login to your patient dashboard
  2. Go to the "Treatment Progress" section
  3. Click "Request Schedule Change"
  4. Select reason and provide details
  5. Submit your request

Direct Contact:

  • Call the hospital directly
  • Speak with your assigned doctor
  • Contact through AyurBot chat

Notice Period:

  • 24 hours advance notice is preferred
  • Emergency rescheduling is always accommodated
  • Multiple rescheduling requests should be discussed with your doctor

Who gives me my login credentials?

Login credentials are provided by different authorities based on your role:

For Patients:

  • Hospital admin creates your account after appointment approval
  • Your assigned doctor provides the login credentials
  • Credentials are shared via phone or email
  • You receive a unique Patient ID and temporary password

For Doctors:

  • Hospital administration creates doctor accounts
  • Admin provides Doctor ID and password
  • Credentials are given during onboarding

For Admins:

  • System administrator creates admin accounts
  • Provided during hospital partnership setup
  • Includes full system access privileges

Security Note: Change your password after first login for security. Never share your credentials with others.

What are the operating hours for appointments?

Operating hours vary by hospital, but generally:

  • Morning: 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Afternoon: 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM
  • Evening: 5:00 PM - 8:00 PM
  • Closed: Sundays and public holidays

Some hospitals may have different timings. Check the specific hospital's profile for exact operating hours and availability.
